Description

The Geological Material Types vocabulary is a SKOS-based hierarchical classification system for geological specimens. The scheme provides comprehensive coverage of the earth sciences domain by accommodating both natural and processed materials, terrestrial and extraterrestrial sources, and the practical needs of collection management across diverse geological institutions.

Meteorite

IRI http://example.com/GeologicMaterialTypes/meteorite
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Astromaterial
Definition A natural, solid object, derived from a celestial body, that was transported by natural means from the body on which it formed to a region outside the dominant gravitational influence of that body and that later collided with a natural or artificial body larger than itself.

Has Broader Rock

Has Broader Fossil
Definition A sedimentary structure produced by the life activities (other than growth) of some organism

Fossil

IRI http://example.com/GeologicMaterialTypes/fossil
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition Any remains, trace, or imprint of a plant or animal preserved in the Earth's crust since some past geologic or prehistoric time; loosely, any evidence of past life.

Rock

IRI http://example.com/GeologicMaterialTypes/rock
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A consolidated or unconsolidated aggregate of minerals consisting of one or more mineral species and having some degree of chemical and mineralogic constancy. Rocks may also contain or consist completely of biological remnants or organic material.
Has Narrower Rock Core

Astromaterial

IRI http://example.com/GeologicMaterialTypes/astromaterial
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A geologic material that is extraterrestrial in origin or derived from a geological process involving an extraterrestrial material (impactite)
Has Narrower Sample Return

Mineral

IRI http://example.com/GeologicMaterialTypes/mineral
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A naturally occurring element or chemical compound of homogeneous composition, normally with a crystalline structure, and resulting from some geological process on Earth or in extraterrestrial bodies

Geological Material

IRI http://example.com/GeologicMaterialTypes/geologicalMaterial
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Alternative Label Geologic Material
