Description

The Geological Sample Material Type vocabulary is a SKOS-based hierarchical classification system primarily for classifying materials in earth sciences collections. The scheme provides comprehensive coverage of the earth sciences domain by accommodating both natural and processed materials, terrestrial and extraterrestrial sources, and the practical needs of collection management across diverse geological institutions.

Concepts

Gemstone

IRI https://linked.data.gov.au/def/geological-sample-type/gemstone
Is Defined By Geological Sample Type vocabulary
Alternative Label Gem
Has Broader Mineral
Definition Any mineral or naturally occurring substance that, when cut and polished, has sufficient beauty, rarity, and durability for use as a personal adornment or other ornament. Organic materials such as pearl are considered gem materials rather than gemstones. Because of their desirability and demand, synthetic and imitation gemstones are common replacements for natural gemstones.

Meteorite

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/meteorite
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Astromaterial
Definition A natural, solid object, derived from a celestial body, that was transported by natural means from the body on which it formed to a region outside the dominant gravitational influence of that body and that later collided with a natural or artificial body larger than itself.

Ore

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/ore
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A naturally occurring geological material from which economically valuable minerals can be extracted.

Rock Core

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/rock-core
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Rock
Definition A geological material, commonly cylindrical in shape, recovered from a borehole through drilling

Sample Return

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/sample-return
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Astromaterial
Definition Astromaterial returned from sample collection missions to celestial objects (e.g. comets, asteroids, the Moon, Mars)

Synthetic Mineral

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/synthetic-mineral
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Mineral
Definition Man-made materials created in a laboratory that are intended to representative of natural occurring geological material

Trace Fossil

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/trace-fossil
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Alternative Label Ichnofossil
Has Broader Fossil
Definition A sedimentary structure produced by the life activities (other than growth) of some organism

Unconsolidated Material

IRI https://w3id.org/gso/1.0/rockmaterial/Unconsolidated_Material
Is Defined By Rock Material Ontology
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A geologic material composed of an aggregation of particles that do not adhere to each other strongly enough that the aggregate can be considered a solid in its own right.

Fossil

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/fossil
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Definition Any remains, trace, or imprint of a plant or animal preserved in the Earth's crust since some past geologic or prehistoric time; loosely, any evidence of past life.
Has Narrower Trace Fossil

Rock

IRI https://w3id.org/gso/1.0/rockmaterial/Rock
Is Defined By Rock Material Ontology
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition Consolidated aggregate of one or more EarthMaterials, or a body of undifferentiated mineral matter, or of solid organic material. Includes mineral aggregates such as granite, shale, marble; glassy matter such as obsidian; and organic material such a coal. Excludes unconsolidated materials.
Has Narrower Rock Core

Astromaterial

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/astromaterial
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A geologic material that is extraterrestrial in origin or derived from a geological process involving an extraterrestrial material (impactite)
Has Narrower Sample Return

Mineral

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/mineral
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Has Broader Geological Material
Definition A naturally occurring element or chemical compound of homogeneous composition, normally with a crystalline structure, and resulting from some geological process on Earth or in extraterrestrial bodies
Has Narrower Synthetic Mineral

Geological Material

IRI https://rs.tdwg.org/vocabularies/geological-specimen-material-types/geological-material
Is Defined By This vocabulary
Alternative Label Material Entity Type
Definition A material entity comprised of a natural amount of matter generated by some geologic process, includes synthetic material intended to represent a geological material
Has Narrower Unconsolidated Material
